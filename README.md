## 🌟 项目简介 | Overview

**GeniusStockAIQuant** 是一个基于中国A股市场的智能量化交易系统。项目利用现有的A股历史数据，结合AI编程工具（如Cursor）的辅助，构建包含**选股、回测、进出场管理**等完整功能的多策略量化交易平台。支持**趋势策略、涨停策略、龙头策略**等多种交易策略。

**GeniusStockAIQuant** is an intelligent quantitative trading system based on the Chinese A-Share market. Leveraging existing A-Share historical data and AI programming tools (such as Cursor), this project builds a comprehensive multi-strategy quantitative trading platform with stock selection, backtesting, and entry/exit management. It supports various trading strategies including trend strategy, limit-up strategy, and leading stock strategy.

## 🎯 核心功能 | Key Features

- ✅ **智能选股系统** | Intelligent Stock Selection
- ✅ **完整回测引擎** | Comprehensive Backtesting Engine
- ✅ **多策略支持** | Multi-Strategy Support (Trend, Limit-up, Leading)
- ✅ **风险管理** | Risk Management & Position Control
- ✅ **性能评估** | Performance Metrics & Visualization
- ✅ **AI辅助开发** | AI-Assisted Development with Cursor

## 📊 主要策略 | Main Strategies

1. **趋势策略** - 基于移动平均线的趋势跟踪
2. **涨停策略** - 涨停板股票预测与交易
3. **龙头策略** - 行业龙头股突破机会
